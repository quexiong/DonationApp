Sunshine Bank
  • About (current)
  • Github Repo (current)

Sunshine Bank is the app that quickly and easily lets you record and archive any cash donations you’ve made. Donated 20 dollars to your church’s youth group? Gave 50 dollars towards your sister’s wedding fund? Contributed 100 dollars to your grandpa’s funeral expenses? Simply create an account and add those donations and any future donations you make. Sunshine Bank will do the rest in keeping a record of your personal donations all in one place.
